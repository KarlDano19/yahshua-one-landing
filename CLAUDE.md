## Design Context

**Product:** YAHSHUA One — AI-native back-office platform for Filipino SMBs (ERP, HR, payroll, accounting, BIR/SSS/PhilHealth/Pag-IBIG compliance).

**Register:** brand (marketing landing page — design IS the product)

**Personality:** Faithful · Intelligent · Bold
- Faithful: earns trust through precision and consistency
- Intelligent: understands the business deeply, speaks in real specifics (₱, BIR forms, cutoff dates)
- Bold: confident, opinionated, proud to be Philippine-made

**Anti-references:** NOT US SaaS (Stripe/Linear adjacents), NOT local gov portal energy, NOT AI hype dashboards, NOT devotional/church aesthetic

**Strategic principles:**
1. Credibility through precision — specific numbers and real examples over generic claims
2. Philippine-native, not Philippine-adapted — compliance context baked in, not bolted on
3. Competence without ceremony — restraint is a trust signal; nothing screams its own importance
4. Show the work, don't claim it — traceable answers, real payroll rows, real BIR references
5. Bold without ego — capable partner, not hype machine
